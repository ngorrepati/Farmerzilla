Farmerzilla

By Nikhil Gorrepati


A web application to let farmers know if it is a good day to plant or harvest depending on the weather and season




Key Features

Harvest

Displays whether or not the next 7 days are good days to harvest your crops



Plant

Displays whether or not the next 7 days are good days to plant your crops
